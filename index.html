Workshop Proposal for CSR'24:

1st International Workshop on Autonomic Computing for Secure Industrial Control Systems and Industrial-IoT 2024

We are proposing to organize 1st International Workshop on Autonomic Computing for Secure Industrial Control Systems and Industrial-IoT (ACSICS'24), in coordination with IEEE Cybersecurity and Resilience (CSR)'24 main conference. We believe this workshop's specific technical focus on security of industrial control systems, operational technology, scada networks and industrial IoT, utilizing autonomic computing, autonomous systems and artificial intelligence is well-aligned with the main theme of the CSR'24 conference. Organizing committee has been researching and actively publishing on Cybersecurity, Autonomic Computing and ICS Security fields.


Introduction

ICS networks stand out as prime targets for global attackers due to their critical role as the lifeblood of any nation's infrastructure. The autonomy and proactive nature of autonomic systems make them inherently preventive, acting as a barrier against potential threats. The supplementary advantage of explainability plays a crucial role in fostering human trust, a particularly vital aspect in an era marked by low confidence in artificial intelligence (AI).

Call For Papers

Topics

In this workshop, we invite the academic and industrial research community in ICS security to submit works related to the field and revolving around the following topic areas:

  • Autonomic Computing (AC) inspired methods for ICS/SCADA security and resiliency.
  • Artificial Intelligence techniques for improving ICS/SCADA security and resiliency.
  • Machine Learning methods for detection and prevention of ICS/SCADA security threats.
  • Application of self-CHOP and self-* properties for secure and resilient ICS/SCADA/OT/IoT/IIoT systems.
  • Autonomous, self-managing security systems for ICS/SCADA.
  • Autonomic security management for ICS/SCADA.
  • ...

The list above is by no means exhaustive, as the aim is to foster the debate around all aspects of autonomic security and resiliency methods for ICS.
