Workshop Proposal for CSR'24:


1st International Workshop on Autonomic Computing for Secure Industrial Control Systems and Industrial-IoT 2024

We are proposing to organize 1st International Workshop on Autonomic Computing for Secure Industrial Control Systems and Industrial-IoT (ACSICS'24), in coordination with IEEE Cybersecurity and Resilience (CSR)'24 main conference. We believe this workshop's specific technical focus on security of industrial control systems, operational technology, scada networks and industrial IoT, utilizing autonomic computing, autonomous systems and artificial intelligence is well-aligned with the main theme of the CSR'24 conference. Organizing committee has been researching and actively publishing on Cybersecurity, Autonomic Computing and ICS Security fields.
